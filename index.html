Garlic Bread

Garlic Bread

Ingredients: 1 French baguette, 1/2 cup butter, 3 garlic cloves (minced), 2 tbsp parsley (chopped), 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions: Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Mix softened butter with minced garlic, parsley and salt. Slice baguette and spread butter mixture on each slice. Wrap in foil and bake for 15 minutes. For crispier bread, open foil and broil for 2-3 minutes.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients: 2 cups basmati rice, 1 lb chicken, 1 cup yogurt, 2 onions, 2 tomatoes, biryani spices

Instructions: Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices. Layer with partially cooked rice. Cook on low heat for 30 minutes. Serve with raita.

Cholle Bhature

Cholle Bhature

Ingredients: 2 cups chickpeas, 3 onions, 3 tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, chole masala, 2 cups flour, 1/2 cup yogurt, 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions: Soak chickpeas overnight. Pressure cook with salt. Prepare masala with onions, tomatoes and spices. For bhature, mix flour, yogurt, baking powder and water to make dough. Roll and deep fry.
